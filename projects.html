Impacting Change with-



NutriLens


An AI-powered tool that helps users understand the nutritional quality of packaged food by uploading a photo of the product's back label. Using advanced vision-language models, NutriLens extracts information and instantly rates the products' healthiness on a scale of 1 to 10, along with a simple explanation.

MindBloom


MindBloom is a website for connecting people to contribute for education of female students. Allows user to create an account, join the volunteering task and share their experiences. It empowers the SDG - quality education for all.
